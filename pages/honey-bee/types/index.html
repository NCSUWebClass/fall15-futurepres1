TYPES OF BEES

Yellow Jackets

Yellow jackets are awful. They sting you and they're mean and aggressive.

No one likes yellow jackets.

Burt's Bees

These bees have been around for millions of years, making them the oldest and wisest of Earth's bee species.

They do not make honey or pollenate flowers. Instead, they apply ancient techniques to craft artisanal beeswax lip balms and facial scrubs.
