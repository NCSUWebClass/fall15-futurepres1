Deinonychus

" Deinonychus was one of the deadliest dinosaurs ever. It had a huge toe claw on each foot. Deinonychus was about the size of the "Velociraptor" that you've seen in Jurassic Park. In fact,the Velociraptor in Jurassic Park should really be Deinonychus. Deinonychus was about 10 feet long, 5 feet tall, and weighed about 200 pounds.

Deinonychus was discovered first near Billings, Montana, in 1931. Barnum Brown first uncovered this fossil with the remains of Tenontosaurus.

Deinonychus was represented in the Dinosaur Train episode, Derek Deinonychus. Also, Deinonychus has been seen in Jurassic Fight Club on National Geographic where a pack of Deinonychus was fighting a Tenontosaurus fro about a day for food."
